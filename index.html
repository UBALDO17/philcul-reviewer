CAR Cordillera Administrative Region Luzon ▾
Key Facts
Only landlocked region in PH.

"Most diversified ethnolinguistic region" in PH.
Tourist spots: Banaue Rice Terraces (Ifugao), Sumaguing Cave (Sagada), Baguio City (Benguet).
