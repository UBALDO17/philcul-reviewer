Only region WITHOUT a province.
Composed of 16 cities + 1 municipality (Pateros).

Main airport: NAIA — between Parañaque & Pasay.
Tourist spots: Rizal Park, National Museum, Intramuros.

Only landlocked region in PH.

"Most diversified ethnolinguistic region" in PH.
Tourist: Banaue Rice Terraces, Sumaguing Cave (Sagada), Baguio City.
